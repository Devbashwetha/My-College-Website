About Our College

Vivekananda Government Degree College in Vidyanagar is a NAAC 'A' grade institution now granted autonomous status. It offers a diverse range of B.A., B.Com., and B.Sc. courses, including specialized programs like BBA and BCA. The campus features modern digital facilities and strong placement support through the Telangana Skill Knowledge Centre. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, it remains a top choice for students seeking quality, flexible government education

History of vivekananda Government Degress Collage

Vivekananda Government Degree College (VGDC) in Vidyanagar, Hyderabad, has a storied history that began in 1966 as an evening college founded by the Swami Vivekananda Education Society. Established under the leadership of Dr. Marri Chenna Reddy and Acharya Khandavalli Lakshmi Ranjanam, its original mission was to provide Telugu-medium education to working students. The institution was taken over by the state government in 1982 and eventually converted into a full-fledged day college in 1990. Over the decades, it evolved from offering basic Arts and Commerce streams to becoming a multi-faculty institution with a wide range of undergraduate programs, including Science and Business Administration. Affiliated with Osmania University, the college achieved a significant milestone by attaining autonomous status for the 2024-2025 academic year, continuing its legacy of providing accessible higher education to diverse socio-economic groups.
