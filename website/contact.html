Vivekananda Government Degree College (VGDC) is a premier co-educational autonomous government institution established in 1966 and located in Vidya Nagar, Hyderabad [1]. Holding a prestigious NAAC 'A' Grade and affiliation with Osmania University, the college delivers budget-friendly, semester-based undergraduate programs across the BBA, BCA, BA, B.Com, and B.Sc streams. Structurally, the curriculum utilizes the Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS) integrated with National Education Policy (NEP) features, allowing student cohorts to manage credit banking via the digital Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) portal. To address real-world corporate demands, the college relies on specialized soft skills and digital training modules run through the Telangana Skill Knowledge Centre (TSKC) and external partnerships with platforms like the Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge (TASK).
